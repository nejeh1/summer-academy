about

Nature is often associated with the aspects of the world that are not created or significantly influenced by human beings. It is the opposite of the human-made or artificial world. Nature includes diverse ecosystems such as forests, mountains, rivers, oceans, deserts, and all the living organisms that inhabit these environments. The study of nature is a branch of science known as natural science. Scientists in fields such as biology, geology, ecology, and meteorology explore and seek to understand the patterns, processes, and interrelationships within nature.
